## How to Build a Productive Desk Setup

![Desk Setup Inspiration]

Creating a productive workspace doesn't require buying everything at once.

Start with these essentials:

### 1. Improve Ergonomics

Use a monitor riser or laptop stand to keep screens at eye level.

### 2. Optimize Lighting

Add a monitor light bar or desk lamp to reduce eye strain.

### 3. Reduce Clutter

Cable management systems and desk mats create a cleaner workspace.

### 4. Upgrade Input Devices

A mechanical keyboard and ergonomic mouse can dramatically improve comfort.

### 5. Automate Your Workflow

Tools like the Stream Deck help streamline repetitive tasks.
